There are about 1.4 million earthquakes every year

Fortunately, the vast majority of them are low in magnitude, occur in uninhabited areas or are centred deep in the Earth’s crust.

Earth Sounds is a visualisation of all earthquakes over the previous day. The events are shown in the order they happened during the day, using the daily data feed from the US Geological Service which is updated every five minutes.
